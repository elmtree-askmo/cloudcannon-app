question: >-
    What topics are covered under the 'Energy Forms' section in the exam?
answer: >-
    In the 'Energy Forms' section of the Fiji Year 8 Basic Science Examination, the following topics are typically covered:\n\n1. **Heat Energy**: Understanding the nature of heat energy, its sources, and its effects on materials.\n2. **Electrical Energy**: Exploring how electrical energy is generated, transferred, and utilized in various applications.\n3. **Sound Energy**: Investigating the properties of sound energy, how it travels, and its impact on the environment.\n4. **Solar Energy**: Examining solar energy as a renewable resource, its conversion into usable forms, and its significance in energy conservation.\n\nThese topics fall under the broader strand of Energy, specifically focusing on energy sources, transfer, transformation, use, and conservation. Students are expected to understand these forms of energy and their practical implications in everyday life.
