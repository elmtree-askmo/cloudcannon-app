    What topics will be covered in the Week 1 class lecture?
answer: >-
    Based on the gathered information, the Week 1 class lecture in a nursing program is likely to cover several foundational topics essential for nursing practice. These topics may include:\n\n1. **Paradigm of Nursing**: An exploration of the fundamental principles and frameworks that guide nursing practice.\n2. **Scope and Standards of Practice**: Discussion on the boundaries of nursing practice and the standards that govern professional conduct.\n3. **Code of Ethics**: An overview of the ethical principles that nurses must adhere to in their practice.\n4. **Nursing Practice Acts**: Examination of the laws and regulations that define nursing practice in various jurisdictions.\n5. **Nursing Process**: Introduction to the systematic approach used by nurses to assess, plan, implement, and evaluate patient care.\n6. **Nursing Theory**: A review of the theoretical frameworks that inform nursing practice and education.\n7. **Health and Wellness Models**: Discussion on various models that define health and wellness and their implications for nursing care.\n\nThese topics are foundational for understanding the role of nurses in promoting and advancing client health, as well as preparing students for more advanced concepts in subsequent weeks. The lecture may also include interactive elements such as flashcards or quizzes to reinforce learning. \n\nIf you need more specific details or additional topics, please let me know!
