question: >-
    What are the new customs duty rates for gold, silver, and platinum?
answer: >-
    In the Union Budget 2024, Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman announced significant reductions in customs duties for precious metals. The new customs duty rates are as follows:\n\n- **Gold**: Reduced to **6%**\n- **Silver**: Reduced to **6%**\n- **Platinum**: Reduced to **6.4%**\n\nThese changes aim to enhance domestic value addition in the gold and precious metal jewelry sector in India. The reductions are part of a broader strategy to support local manufacturing and improve the competitiveness of exports.
