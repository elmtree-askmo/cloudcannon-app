question: >-
    What are the key road projects currently underway in Bihar?
answer: >-
    In the Union Budget 2024, significant investments were announced for road infrastructure development in Bihar, totaling Rs 26,000 crore. This funding aims to enhance road connectivity and includes several key projects:\n\n1. **Patna-Purnea Expressway**: This expressway is a major project aimed at improving connectivity between the capital city of Patna and Purnea, facilitating better transportation and economic activities in the region.\n\n2. **Buxar-Bhagalpur Road**: Another important project that is part of the government's initiative to enhance road infrastructure in Bihar, aimed at improving access between these two significant towns.\n\n3. **Additional Road Projects**: The budget also includes allocations for various other road projects that are expected to boost overall infrastructure in the state.\n\nFurthermore, the Bihar government is also working on several rural infrastructure projects, with Chief Minister Nitish Kumar launching road and bridge projects worth Rs 8,837.77 crore to enhance rural connectivity.\n\nOverall, these initiatives reflect the government's commitment to improving transportation infrastructure in Bihar, which is crucial for the state's economic development and connectivity.
