question: >-
    What is the allocation for the Defence Ministry in the Union Budget 2024?
answer: >-
    In the Union Budget 2024-25, the Defence Ministry has been allocated ₹6,21,940 crore (approximately $75 billion), which represents a 4.79% increase from the previous financial year. This allocation is the highest among all ministries and constitutes nearly 13% of the government's total expenditure. \n\nThe budget emphasizes capital acquisition, with ₹1.72 lakh crore (about 27.67% of the total defence budget) earmarked for capital expenditure. Additionally, the budget for revenue expenditure (excluding salaries) is set at ₹92,088 crore, while the allocation for defence pensions has increased to ₹1.41 lakh crore. \n\nThis increase in the defence budget aims to bolster military capabilities, enhance infrastructure, and support domestic procurement initiatives.
