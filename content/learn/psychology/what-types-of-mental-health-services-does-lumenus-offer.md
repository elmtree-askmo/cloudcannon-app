question: >-
    What types of mental health services does Lumenus offer?
answer: >-
    Lumenus Community Services offers a comprehensive range of mental health services tailored to children, youth, families, and individuals in Toronto. The key services provided by Lumenus include:\n\n1. **Counseling Services**: Individual and family counseling tailored to the specific needs of clients, addressing various mental health issues.\n\n2. **Children's Mental Health Programs**: Programs specifically designed to support the mental health of children, including early intervention services.\n\n3. **Youth Mental Health Programs**: Targeted services for youth, including counseling and support in various settings.\n\n4. **Autism Services**: Specialized support programs for individuals with autism, focusing on developmental and mental health needs.\n\n5. **Urgent Response Service**: A service designed to provide immediate support in crisis situations.\n\n6. **Wraparound Supports**: Comprehensive support that includes various community resources to assist families and individuals.\n\n7. **School Programs**: Mental health counseling and support services integrated within school settings to help students.\n\n8. **Speech and Language Program**: Services aimed at addressing communication challenges that may affect mental health and development.\n\n9. **Access to Residential Services**: Options for residential treatment for those requiring more intensive support.\n\n10. **Day Treatment Programs**: Structured programs that provide therapeutic support during the day while allowing individuals to return home in the evenings.\n\nLumenus is committed to providing accessible and integrated mental health services, ensuring that individuals and families receive the support they need in a community-based setting. For more information, you can contact Lumenus at 1-833-LUMENUS (586-3687) or visit their website.
