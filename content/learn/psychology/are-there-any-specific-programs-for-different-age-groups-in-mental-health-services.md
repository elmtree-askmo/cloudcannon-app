question: >-
    Are there any specific programs for different age groups in mental health services?
answer: >-
    Lumenus offers a variety of mental health services tailored to different age groups, ensuring that individuals receive appropriate support based on their developmental needs. Here’s an overview of the specific programs available for various age groups:\n\n1. **Infants and Young Children**: Lumenus provides early childhood development services, including EarlyON and Child and Family Centres, which focus on the developmental needs of infants and young children.\n\n2. **Children**: The organization offers specialized Children’s Mental Health Programs and Services, which cater to the unique challenges faced by children. This includes tailored individual and family counseling, as well as school programs designed to support children with mental health concerns.\n\n3. **Youth (Ages 12-25)**: Lumenus has a robust set of programs for youth, including:\n   - **Central Toronto Youth Wellness Hub**: This hub provides integrated mental health and substance use services for young people aged 12-25.\n   - **Wellness Drop-In Centre**: A free service for youth aged 12-25 that integrates mental health and substance use support.\n   - **Doorways for Transitional Age Youth**: This program offers mental health and substance use counseling and case management for youth aged 15-25 and their caregivers.\n   - **The What's Up Walk-In® Clinic**: This clinic provides free mental health counseling for children, youth, young adults, and families, addressing issues such as anxiety, depression, and behavioral concerns.\n\n4. **Families**: Lumenus also offers family counseling services, which are designed to support families dealing with mental health issues affecting their members, regardless of age.\n\n5. **Specialized Programs**: For individuals with autism, Lumenus provides autism support programs that cater to children and youth, ensuring that they receive the necessary support for their developmental and mental health needs.\n\nOverall, Lumenus aims to create a seamless and integrated network of services that address the mental health needs of individuals across all age groups, from infants to young adults, ensuring that everyone receives the personalized guidance and support they require.
