question: >-
    Are there any eligibility criteria for accessing Lumenus's services?
answer: >-
    Lumenus Community Services has specific eligibility criteria for accessing its various programs and services, which primarily cater to children, youth, and families. Here are some key points regarding eligibility:\n\n1. **General Age Range**: Most services are designed for children and youth aged 0-18 years. This includes mental health services, developmental services, and autism support programs.\n\n2. **Specific Programs**:\n   - **Urgent Response Service**: This service is suitable for youth aged 13-18 years who require residential placement due to family breakdown, physical or sexual abuse, emotional trauma, or mental illness.\n   - **Autism Services**: Families interested in autism services must contact Lumenus directly to determine eligibility, especially for the Autism Fee for Service program. The program is available for clients diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) by a qualified professional.\n\n3. **Legal Guardian Involvement**: For many services, a legal guardian must contact the center directly to inquire about eligibility and to initiate the process.\n\n4. **Crisis Situations**: Programs like the Partners in Autism program are available for clients aged 12-18 who are facing imminent crises due to complex needs and behaviors.\n\n5. **Residency Requirements**: Some services may have specific residency requirements, such as being located within certain geographical boundaries (e.g., north of Steeles Ave W to Hwy 401, and west of Humber River to Dufferin St).\n\nFor detailed information regarding eligibility for specific programs, it is recommended to contact Lumenus directly or visit their website. The contact information for Lumenus is as follows:\n\n- **Phone**: 1-833-LUMENUS (586-3687)\n- **Email**: info@lumenus.ca\n- **Address**: 1126 Finch Avenue West, Unit 16, Toronto, ON, M3J 3J6\n\nThis information should help potential clients understand the eligibility criteria for accessing Lumenus's services.
