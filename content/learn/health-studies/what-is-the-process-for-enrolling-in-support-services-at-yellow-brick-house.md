question: >-
    What is the process for enrolling in support services at Yellow Brick House?
answer: >-
    To enroll in support services at Yellow Brick House, individuals can follow these steps:\n\n1. **Contact the Crisis Line**: For immediate assistance, it is recommended to call or text the toll-free and confidential crisis and support line at 1-800-263-3247. This line is available 24/7 and can provide advice on the next steps.\n\n2. **Intake Assessment**: After reaching out, an intake assessment will be conducted by Yellow Brick House. This assessment helps determine the specific needs of the individual and the appropriate services that can be provided.\n\n3. **Eligibility**: Yellow Brick House offers support primarily for women and children who are experiencing or have experienced domestic violence. The services are tailored to meet the unique circumstances of each individual.\n\n4. **Additional Support**: Once enrolled, individuals can access a range of services including emergency shelter, counseling, legal advocacy, and transitional housing, among others.\n\nFor further inquiries or to get more information about the services offered, individuals can also visit the Yellow Brick House website or contact their administrative office at 905-709-0900.\n\nIt is important to note that if someone is in immediate danger, they should call 911 for emergency assistance.
