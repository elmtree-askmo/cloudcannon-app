question: >-
    What specialized programs does Lumenus offer for autism support?
answer: >-
    Lumenus offers a variety of specialized programs for autism support, aimed at meeting the individual needs of children, youth, and their families. Key programs include:\n\n1. **Partners in Autism Program**: This program is designed for clients aged 12-18 who are facing imminent crises due to complex needs and behaviors associated with autism. It provides a circle of support that includes a Lumenus Service Coordinator and a consultant from Kerryâ€™s Place Autism Services. This program focuses on assisting both the clients and their parents/guardians/caregivers.\n\n2. **Autism Services for Children Aged 2-12**: Lumenus provides a range of services specifically tailored for younger children diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). These services include:\n   - **Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) Therapy**: A widely recognized approach that uses techniques and principles to bring about meaningful and positive changes in behavior.\n   - **Speech Therapy**: Support for improving communication skills, which is often a challenge for children with autism.\n   - **Social Skills Training**: Programs designed to help children develop the necessary skills to interact effectively with peers and adults.\n\n3. **Life Skills and Communication Groups**: These groups aim to create a safe and supportive environment for children to enhance their life skills and communication abilities.\n\nLumenus emphasizes the use of evidence-based tools and services to ensure optimal learning and development for children and youth with autism, while also providing support to their families.
