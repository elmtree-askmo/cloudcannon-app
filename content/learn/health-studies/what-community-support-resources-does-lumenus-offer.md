question: >-
    What community support resources does Lumenus offer?
answer: >-
    Lumenus offers a comprehensive range of community support resources aimed at addressing the mental health and developmental needs of children, youth, families, and individuals across Toronto. Some of the key community support resources provided by Lumenus include:\n\n1. **Urgent Response Service**: This service provides immediate support for individuals in crisis, ensuring timely intervention.\n\n2. **Wraparound Supports**: These are tailored services designed to meet the unique needs of families and individuals, facilitating a holistic approach to care.\n\n3. **EarlyON Child and Family Centres**: These centres offer programs and services for young children and their families, focusing on early childhood development.\n\n4. **Childrenâ€™s Mental Health Programs and Services**: A variety of programs aimed at supporting the mental health of children, including individual and family counselling.\n\n5. **Autism Services**: Lumenus provides specialized support for children and youth diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder (ASD), including evidence-based tools and services.\n\n6. **Youth Mental Health Programs and Services**: These programs cater specifically to the mental health needs of youth, including the Wellness drop-in centre for ages 12-25, which integrates mental health and substance use support.\n\n7. **School Programs**: Lumenus collaborates with schools to provide mental health support and resources for students.\n\n8. **Speech and Language Program**: This program assists children with communication challenges, promoting their language development.\n\n9. **2SLGBTQ+ Programs and Supports**: Lumenus offers resources and support tailored to the needs of 2SLGBTQ+ youth and families.\n\n10. **Residential Treatment and Resources**: For individuals requiring more intensive support, Lumenus provides access to residential services.\n\n11. **Doorways Program**: This program offers mental health and substance use counselling, as well as case management services for youth aged 15-25 and their caregivers, particularly in the Jane Finch community and the North West area of Toronto.\n\n12. **Griffin Community Support Network**: A network that provides additional community support resources.\n\nThese resources are designed to foster mental well-being, support developmental needs, and enhance the overall quality of life for individuals and families in the community. For more information or to access these services, individuals can contact Lumenus directly.
