question: >-
    What is the process for accessing the Red Door Family Shelter's services?
answer: >-
    To access the services of the Red Door Family Shelter, individuals or families in need must go through a specific application process. Here are the steps to follow:\n\n1. **Contact Central Intake**: The first step is to reach out to Central Intake for family shelter referrals. This can be done by calling either of the following numbers:\n   - 416-338-4766\n   - 1-877-338-3398\n\n2. **Assessment of Availability**: Upon contacting Central Intake, they will assess the current availability of shelter space at the Red Door Family Shelter. If there are no available beds, they will assist in finding another suitable shelter.\n\n3. **Eligibility Criteria**: The Red Door Family Shelter primarily serves families who are homeless, including:\n   - Immigrant families awaiting hearing or status\n   - Government-sponsored or refugee claimant families\n   - Women and their children fleeing domestic abuse\n\n4. **24/7 Support**: The Red Door Family Shelter operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week, ensuring that assistance is available at any time.\n\n5. **Additional Resources**: For further support, individuals can also call 2-1-1 to find social services, financial assistance programs, or community supports available in their area.\n\nBy following these steps, individuals and families can access the necessary support and services provided by the Red Door Family Shelter.
