question: >-
    How can individuals contact Lumenus for services or inquiries?
answer: >-
    Individuals can contact Lumenus Community Services for services or inquiries through the following methods:\n\n1. **Phone**: You can reach Lumenus at 1-833-LUMENUS (586-3687).\n\n2. **Email**: For general inquiries, you can email them at info@lumenus.ca.\n\n3. **Physical Address**: If you prefer to visit or send mail, the head office is located at:\n   ```\n   Lumenus Community Services\n   1126 Finch Avenue West, Unit 16\n   Toronto, Ontario, M3J 3J6\n   Canada\n   ```\n\n4. **Online Contact Form**: Lumenus also provides an online contact form on their website, where you can fill out your details and someone will get back to you within 1-2 business days.\n\n5. **Specialized Programs**: For specific programs, such as the Autism Fee for Service program, you can contact the Service Program Coordinator directly at 416-744-7650, ext. 4262 or via email at autism.intake@lumenus.ca.\n\nThese contact options ensure that individuals can easily reach out for support, information, or assistance regarding the various services offered by Lumenus.
