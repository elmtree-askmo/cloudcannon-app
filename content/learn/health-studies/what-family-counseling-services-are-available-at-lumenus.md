question: >-
    What family counseling services are available at Lumenus?
answer: >-
    Lumenus Community Services offers a range of family counseling services aimed at supporting children, youth, young adults, and their families in Toronto. These services are designed to address various challenges related to early years, developmental issues, mental health, and autism. \n\nThe family counseling services provided by Lumenus include:\n\n1. **Individual and Family Counseling**: Lumenus offers personalized counseling sessions that cater to the specific needs of families and individuals facing mental health challenges.\n\n2. **Community-Based Programs**: These programs are integrated with mental health support and are designed to foster emotional growth and development within families.\n\n3. **Urgent Response Service**: This service provides immediate support for families in crisis, ensuring that they receive timely assistance.\n\n4. **Wraparound Supports**: Lumenus offers comprehensive support that wraps around the family, addressing multiple needs and ensuring a holistic approach to care.\n\n5. **Youth Mental Health Programs**: These programs specifically target the mental health needs of youth, providing them with the necessary tools and support.\n\n6. **Access to Additional Resources**: Families can also benefit from various community support resources that Lumenus connects them with, enhancing their overall well-being.\n\nFor more information or to access these services, families can contact Lumenus at their main office located at 1126 Finch Avenue West, Unit 16, Toronto, ON, M3J 3J6, or by calling 1 833 LUMENUS (586 3687).
