question: >-
    What are the main topics covered in the Multiple-Choice Question section?
answer: >-
    The Multiple-Choice Question section of the Fiji Year 8 Healthy Living Examination typically covers a variety of topics relevant to health education. Based on the gathered information, the main topics included in this section are:\n\n1. **Nutrition**: Questions may focus on the importance of nutrition in health promotion and the nutritional challenges faced by emerging populations.\n2. **Adolescent Changes**: This includes questions related to puberty and the physical and emotional changes that occur during adolescence.\n3. **Mental Health**: Topics may cover aspects of mental well-being, peer pressure, and relationships among adolescents.\n4. **Gender Roles**: Questions may address societal expectations and stereotypes related to gender.\n5. **Respiration**: Basic understanding of the respiratory system and its functions may be tested.\n6. **Group Behaviors**: This could include questions about social dynamics and behaviors within peer groups.\n\nThe Multiple-Choice section consists of twenty compulsory questions, contributing a total of 40 marks to the overall examination score. Each question is designed to assess students' understanding of these critical health topics, which are essential for promoting healthy living among young individuals.
