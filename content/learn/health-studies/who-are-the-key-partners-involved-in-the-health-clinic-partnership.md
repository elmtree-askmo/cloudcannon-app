question: >-
    Who are the key partners involved in the Health Clinic Partnership?
answer: >-
    The key partners involved in the Health Clinic Partnership associated with the Anne Maria D’Amico Foundation include the North York Women's Shelter (NYWS) and the Black Creek Community Health Centre. This partnership aims to support a primary health care clinic that provides essential medical care, harm reduction support, counseling, and system navigation for shelter residents and community members seeking services related to violence against women. The collaboration between these organizations is crucial in delivering comprehensive wrap-around services to women experiencing violence in the North York community.
