    Name two reliable sources of nutritional information online.
answer: >-
    Two reliable sources of nutritional information online are:\n\n1. **Nutritionvalue.org**: This website provides comprehensive nutritional information about various foods, including calorie content and macromolecule breakdown. It is user-friendly and allows individuals to search for specific foods to obtain detailed nutritional data.\n\n2. **Tools.myfooddata.com**: This online tool enables users to search for specific foods and access detailed information on their calorie content and the breakdown of macromolecules. It is a valuable resource for tracking dietary intake and understanding the nutritional value of different foods.\n\nBoth of these sources are useful for anyone looking to manage their dietary intake and understand the nutritional content of the foods they consume.
