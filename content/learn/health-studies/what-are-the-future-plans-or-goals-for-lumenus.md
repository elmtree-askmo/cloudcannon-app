question: >-
    What are the future plans or goals for Lumenus?
answer: >-
    Lumenus has outlined several future plans and goals as part of its strategic plan for 2022-2025. The organization aims to grow as a unified entity that focuses on enhancing internal strengths, improving client care, and fostering collaboration within the mental health and community service systems. Here are some key objectives from their strategic plan:\n\n1. **Improving Client Pathways**: Lumenus is committed to optimizing the client experience from the first point of contact. This includes working with partners to create seamless services and minimize gaps in access to care, ensuring that clients receive high-quality support.\n\n2. **Equitable Access to Care**: The organization aims to provide seamless and equitable access to mental health and developmental services. This involves creating a guided network of services that reduces systemic barriers and ensures that every individual and family in Toronto can access the support they need.\n\n3. **Client and Family Involvement**: Lumenus plans to include clients and their families as key partners in the design of care and services. This collaborative approach is intended to enhance the relevance and effectiveness of the support provided.\n\n4. **Investment in Staff and Community**: The organization recognizes the importance of investing time, talent, and resources to ensure that both clients and staff feel a sense of belonging. This includes initiatives aimed at fostering diversity, equity, and inclusion within the organization.\n\n5. **Expansion of Virtual Support**: As the demand for mental health services increases, Lumenus is focusing on expanding its virtual support offerings. This is particularly important for maintaining connectivity for vulnerable communities, especially in light of the growing reliance on technology for mental health support.\n\nOverall, Lumenus is dedicated to building a brighter future for its clients by enhancing service delivery, fostering community connections, and ensuring that mental health services are accessible to all.
