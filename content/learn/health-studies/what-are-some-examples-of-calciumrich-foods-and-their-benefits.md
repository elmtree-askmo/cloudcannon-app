question: >-
    What are some examples of calcium-rich foods and their benefits?
answer: >-
    Calcium is an essential mineral that plays a crucial role in various bodily functions, including building and maintaining strong bones and teeth, supporting nerve function, muscle health, and hormone regulation. Here are some examples of calcium-rich foods along with their benefits:\n\n1. **Dairy Products**:\n   - **Milk**: A cup of skim milk provides about 325 mg of calcium, which is approximately 25% of the daily value (DV).\n   - **Yogurt**: One cup of plain yogurt contains around 300 mg of calcium, providing about 23% of the DV.\n   - **Cheese**: Cheddar cheese offers about 15% of the DV for calcium in a one-ounce serving.\n\n2. **Fish**:\n   - **Sardines**: A can of sardines contains approximately 351 mg of calcium and is also a good source of protein.\n   - **Canned Salmon**: This fish is another excellent source of calcium, especially when the bones are included.\n\n3. **Legumes and Beans**:\n   - **White Beans**: One cup provides about 161 mg of calcium and is rich in protein.\n   - **Edamame**: A cup of frozen edamame offers about 5% of the DV for calcium.\n\n4. **Nuts and Seeds**:\n   - **Almonds**: One cup of whole almonds contains about 385 mg of calcium, which is more than one-third of the recommended daily amount.\n   - **Chia Seeds**: These seeds are not only high in calcium but also contain nutrients that help metabolize calcium.\n\n5. **Leafy Greens**:\n   - **Collard Greens**: One cup of raw collard greens provides about 84 mg of calcium.\n   - **Mustard Greens**: A cup contains around 64 mg of calcium.\n   - **Kale**: Another leafy green that contributes to calcium intake.\n\n6. **Fortified Foods**:\n   - **Fortified Soy Milk**: One cup can provide about 300 mg of calcium, making it a great alternative for those who are lactose intolerant or vegan.\n   - **Fortified Juices**: Some fruit juices are fortified with calcium, providing an additional source.\n\n7. **Other Sources**:\n   - **Tofu**: Calcium-set tofu can be a significant source of calcium, depending on how it is prepared.\n   - **Dried Figs**: These fruits are not only delicious but also provide a good amount of calcium.\n\nIncorporating a variety of these calcium-rich foods into your diet can help ensure you meet your calcium needs, which is vital for maintaining bone health and preventing deficiencies.
