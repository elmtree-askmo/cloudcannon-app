question: >-
    Does Lumenus collaborate with other organizations for community support?
answer: >-
    Yes, Lumenus Community Services actively collaborates with other organizations to enhance community support. One notable example is the formation of the "What's Up" program, which is a collaboration among four agencies that provides free mental health counseling to children, youth, young adults, and their families. This program was established in 2011 and exemplifies Lumenus's commitment to working with various partners to deliver essential mental health services.\n\nAdditionally, Lumenus prioritizes collaboration with clients, families, and other organizations to create tailored solutions that foster a supportive community environment. They regularly engage with staff and guests from other organizations during meetings to share information about programs and solicit feedback, further emphasizing their collaborative approach.\n\nLumenus also partners with organizations like VitalHub to integrate advanced technology into their service delivery, which is a critical component of their strategic plan. This partnership, along with others, helps Lumenus to reduce obstacles to care and close gaps between services, making it one of the largest mental health and developmental services agencies in the province.\n\nOverall, Lumenus's collaborative efforts are integral to their mission of promoting wellness and improving access to mental health services in the Toronto area.
