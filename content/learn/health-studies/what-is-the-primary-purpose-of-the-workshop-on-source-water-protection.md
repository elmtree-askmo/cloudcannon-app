question: >-
    What is the primary purpose of the workshop on source water protection?
answer: >-
    The primary purpose of the workshop on source water protection is to discuss coordination opportunities and challenges related to the Clean Water Act (CWA) and the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA). The workshop aims to identify next steps to better protect sources of drinking water, which include both groundwater and surface water, and to improve overall water quality. \n\nKey objectives of the workshop include:\n\n1. Developing a comprehensive plan that outlines affected areas, including creating maps.\n2. Identifying and ranking potential threats to source water.\n3. Engaging the public in the source water protection process.\n4. Providing participants with resources related to source water assessment and protection.\n\nThe workshop serves as a platform for stakeholders to collaborate, share knowledge, and develop actionable strategies to safeguard drinking water sources effectively.
