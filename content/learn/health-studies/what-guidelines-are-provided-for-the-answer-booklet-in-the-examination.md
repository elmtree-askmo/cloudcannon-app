question: >-
    What guidelines are provided for the Answer Booklet in the examination?
answer: >-
    The guidelines provided for the Answer Booklet in the Fiji Year 8 Examination include the following instructions:\n\n1. **Index Number**: Students must write their Index Number in the space provided in the Answer Booklet.\n2. **Ink Color**: All answers should be written in the Answer Booklet using either blue or black ink. The use of red ink is prohibited.\n\nThese instructions are consistent across various subjects in the Year 8 examinations, ensuring clarity and uniformity in the examination process.
