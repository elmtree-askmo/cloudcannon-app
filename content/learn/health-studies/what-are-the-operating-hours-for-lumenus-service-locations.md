    What are the operating hours for Lumenus service locations?
answer: >-
    As of September 3rd, 2024, Lumenus service locations have specific operating hours for in-person and virtual sessions. The in-person walk-in sessions for children and youth aged 0-26 are available from Tuesday to Friday. \n\nFor general hours of operation, Lumenus Community Services typically operates as follows:\n\n- **Monday to Friday**: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM\n- **Tuesday to Thursday**: Extended hours may be available, often until 8:00 PM on certain days.\n\nIt's important to note that these hours may vary by specific service location, so it is advisable to check directly with the Lumenus service location you plan to visit for the most accurate and up-to-date information.
