question: >-
    What is the capacity of the Red Door Family Shelter's emergency shelter?
answer: >-
    The Red Door Family Shelter has a capacity of 106 individuals in its emergency shelter. This facility provides essential services such as meals, counseling, support, orientation, interpretation, referrals, medical support, outreach, and housing support. The length of stay in the shelter is determined based on individual needs, allowing families to receive the necessary support during their time of crisis.
