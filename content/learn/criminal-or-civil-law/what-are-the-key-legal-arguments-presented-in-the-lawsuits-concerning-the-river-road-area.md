The plaintiff lost a lawsuit regarding this automatic approval, suggesting that legal arguments may focus on procedural fairness and the adequacy of public input in the planning process.\n\n3. **Community Opposition**: There is a clear indication of community activism against certain development projects, such as the proposed four-story residential project near the Hudson River.
