question: >-
    What were the major reactions and tributes following Sitaram Yechury's passing?
answer: >-
    Following the passing of Sitaram Yechury, the general secretary of the Communist Party of India (Marxist), at the age of 72, there was a significant outpouring of tributes and condolences from various political leaders and parties across the spectrum. His death, which occurred on September 12, 2024, after a prolonged illness, marked the end of an era for the CPI(M) and the leftist movement in India.\n\n1. **Political Leaders' Reactions**: Many political figures expressed their sorrow and paid tribute to Yechury's contributions to Indian politics. For instance, leaders highlighted his role as a staunch advocate for labor, workers, and farmers' rights, emphasizing that his passing silenced a vital voice in these movements. Notably, Maharashtra's Chief Minister, Sharad Pawar, remarked on the loss of one of the strongest pillars of the leftist movement, reflecting on their collaborative efforts in the past.\n\n2. **Condolences from the CPI(M)**: The CPI(M) itself announced that details regarding public tributes would be shared soon, indicating the party's intention to honor Yechury's legacy. The party described him as a beloved comrade whose commitment to Marxist principles was unwavering.\n\n3. **Memorial Services**: A memorial meeting was held at the party headquarters, AKG Bhavan, where Yechury's mortal remains were brought. This gathering served as a poignant reminder of his enduring legacy and his relentless fight for the underprivileged. Telangana Chief Minister Revanth Reddy noted the immense and irreplaceable loss for the Left movement, especially in the current political climate.\n\n4. **Legacy and Impact**: Yechury was remembered not only for his political acumen but also for his ability to bridge divides and unite diverse political forces against communalism. His contributions to Indian politics and public service were lauded by leaders from various parties, underscoring the respect he commanded across political lines.\n\nIn summary, Sitaram Yechury's passing elicited heartfelt tributes that celebrated his significant impact on Indian politics, his commitment to social justice, and his role as a unifying figure in the leftist movement.
