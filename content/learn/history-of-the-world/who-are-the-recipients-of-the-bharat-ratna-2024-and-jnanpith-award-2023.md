question: >-
    Who are the recipients of the Bharat Ratna 2024 and Jnanpith Award 2023?
answer: >-
    As of 2024, the recipients of the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor, are:\n\n1. **Karpoori Thakur** (1924-1988) - A prominent political leader and former Chief Minister of Bihar.\n2. **Lal Krishna Advani** - A senior leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and a key figure in Indian politics.\n3. **Narasimha Rao** - Former Prime Minister of India, known for his role in liberalizing the Indian economy.\n4. **Chaudhary Charan Singh** - Former Prime Minister of India, recognized for his contributions to agrarian policies.\n5. **M. S. Swaminathan** (1925-2023) - Renowned agricultural scientist and a key figure in India's Green Revolution.\n\nIn addition, the 58th Jnanpith Award for the year 2023 has been awarded to two distinguished writers:\n\n1. **Jagatguru Shri Rambhadracharya** - An eminent Sanskrit scholar and educator.\n2. **Gulzar** - A famous Urdu poet, lyricist, and filmmaker.\n\nThese awards highlight the significant contributions of these individuals to Indian society and culture.
