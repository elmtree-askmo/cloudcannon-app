question: >-
    What was the intensity and impact of the Israeli airstrikes in Beirut?
answer: >-
    The intensity and impact of the Israeli airstrikes in Beirut have been significant and devastating. Recent reports indicate that these airstrikes have resulted in the deaths of at least 20 people and have injured dozens more in central Beirut, marking one of the deadliest attacks on the city since the onset of this military campaign. The airstrikes have been characterized by their suddenness, with Lebanese officials noting that these attacks, once rare, have now become more frequent and occur without warning.\n\nIn addition to the immediate casualties, the airstrikes have caused extensive destruction in the affected areas. For instance, a notable airstrike destroyed a four-story building in the heart of Beirut, leading to a massive trail of destruction across the city and its suburbs. The Lebanese health ministry has reported that the overall toll from the airstrikes in Lebanon has been staggering, with over 1,400 people killed and nearly 7,500 injured, alongside the displacement of more than one million individuals from their homes.\n\nThe airstrikes are part of a broader military strategy by Israel, which has included the use of heavy munitions, contributing to the high death toll and widespread destruction. The situation remains tense, with ongoing military actions and a backdrop of political and military responses from groups such as Hezbollah, which has vowed to continue its operations against Israel until a ceasefire is reached.\n\nOverall, the Israeli airstrikes in Beirut have had a profound impact on the civilian population, leading to loss of life, injuries, and significant infrastructural damage, while also escalating the ongoing conflict in the region.
