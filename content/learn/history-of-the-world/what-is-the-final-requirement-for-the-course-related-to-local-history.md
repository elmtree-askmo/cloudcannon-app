question: >-
    What is the final requirement for the course related to local history?
answer: >-
    The final requirement for the course related to local history, specifically in the context of "Readings in Philippine History," involves students being grouped to submit a project that showcases Philippine indigenous arts or artifacts. This requirement is part of the Course Learning Outcome aimed at manifesting interest in local history and promoting the preservation of the country's cultural heritage.\n\nAdditionally, students may also be tasked with creating an engaging and informative video on a specific local history topic, such as the history of a particular locality, which further emphasizes the importance of local heritage in the broader context of Philippine history. This approach encourages students to actively engage with their local history and contribute to its documentation and promotion. \n\nOverall, the final requirements are designed to foster a deeper understanding of local history and its significance within the national narrative.
