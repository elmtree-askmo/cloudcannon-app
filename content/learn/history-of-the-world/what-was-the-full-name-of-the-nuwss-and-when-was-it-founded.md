question: >-
    What was the full name of the NUWSS and when was it founded?
answer: >-
    The full name of the NUWSS is the National Union of Women's Suffrage Societies. It was founded in 1897 in the United Kingdom to unite various women's suffrage organizations and advocate for women's right to vote through peaceful and legal means. The NUWSS played a significant role in the women's rights movement and was instrumental in promoting suffrage across different social classes. In 1919, it was renamed the National Union of Societies for Equal Citizenship.
