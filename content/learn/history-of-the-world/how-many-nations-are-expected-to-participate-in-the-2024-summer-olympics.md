question: >-
    How many nations are expected to participate in the 2024 Summer Olympics?
answer: >-
    The 2024 Summer Olympics, officially known as the Games of the XXXIII Olympiad, are expected to feature delegations from 206 countries. While not every nation in the world will be represented, most countries typically send at least one competitor. Approximately 10,500 athletes are anticipated to participate in the Games, which will take place in Paris, France, from July 26 to August 11, 2024. This event will mark a significant milestone as it aims for a gender balance, with an equal split of 50% men and 50% women athletes competing.
