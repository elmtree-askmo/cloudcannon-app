question: >-
    Where and when did the Quad Summit take place?
answer: >-
    The 2023 Quad Leaders' Summit took place in Hiroshima, Japan, on 20 May 2023. This summit was convened by Australia and included the participation of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi of India, Prime Minister Kishida Fumio of Japan, and President Joseph R. Biden Jr. of the United States. The leaders discussed their vision for an open, stable, and prosperous Indo-Pacific during this meeting.
