question: >-
    Which countries are currently involved in the Russia-Ukraine conflict?
answer: >-
    The countries currently involved in the Russia-Ukraine conflict primarily include:\n\n1. **Russia**: The aggressor in the conflict, having invaded Ukraine on February 24, 2022, escalating the ongoing tensions that began in 2014.\n2. **Ukraine**: The nation defending its sovereignty and territorial integrity against the Russian invasion.\n3. **United States**: A significant supporter of Ukraine, providing extensive military and financial aid, amounting to over $44 billion in materiel aid as of November 2023.\n4. **European Union (EU) Member States**: Various EU countries have also provided military and humanitarian support to Ukraine.\n5. **NATO Countries**: While Ukraine is not a NATO member, many NATO countries have supported Ukraine through military aid and training.\n6. **North Korea**: Allegedly involved by providing troops to support Russia, in exchange for military equipment from Russia.\n7. **Other countries**: Nations like India, South Africa, Brazil, and Turkey have participated in discussions regarding peace processes, indicating their involvement in diplomatic efforts related to the conflict.\n\nThe conflict has drawn a wide range of international responses, with some countries taking a more ambiguous stance or not commenting at all. The situation remains complex, with ongoing military engagements and geopolitical implications.
