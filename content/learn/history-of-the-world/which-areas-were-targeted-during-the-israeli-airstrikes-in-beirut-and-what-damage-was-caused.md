question: >-
    Which areas were targeted during the Israeli airstrikes in Beirut and what damage was caused?
answer: >-
    The recent Israeli airstrikes in Beirut have targeted several key areas, resulting in significant damage. Notably, the strikes have hit an upscale neighborhood along Beirut's seafront, which is home to the American University of Beirut. Additionally, the Basta neighborhood was specifically mentioned as being heavily affected, where a four-story building was destroyed during an airstrike.\n\nThe airstrikes have caused widespread destruction, with reports of giant explosions and plumes of smoke rising from the impacted areas. Rescuers have been seen sifting through the rubble in search of victims or survivors, indicating the severity of the damage. The strikes have also affected the vicinity of Hiram Hospital in Tyre, southern Lebanon, leading to damage to the hospital building and courtyard.\n\nOverall, the airstrikes have resulted in significant destruction in the heart of Beirut and its southern suburbs, with residents witnessing the aftermath of the bombings and returning to assess the damage after a ceasefire was reached between Israel and Hezbollah.
