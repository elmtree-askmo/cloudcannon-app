question: >-
    What countries are participating in the Indian Air Force Multilateral Exercise Tarang Shakti?
answer: >-
    The Indian Air Force (IAF) is set to host its largest-ever multilateral exercise, Tarang Shakti, which will take place in two phases. The first phase is scheduled from August 6 to 14, 2024, at Sulur Air Force Station in Tamil Nadu, followed by the second phase from August 29 to September 14, 2024, at Jodhpur Air Force Station in Rajasthan.\n\nIn total, 11 countries will participate with military assets, including:\n\n- Australia\n- France\n- Germany\n- Greece\n- Japan\n- Singapore\n- Spain\n- Sri Lanka\n- United Arab Emirates (UAE)\n- United Kingdom (UK)\n- United States (US)\n\nAdditionally, 18 other countries will participate as observers, making the exercise a significant event for international military cooperation and interoperability. The exercise aims to showcase India's defense capabilities and strengthen partnerships among the participating nations, particularly in the context of a Free and Open Indo-Pacific.
