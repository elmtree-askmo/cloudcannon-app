Visitors can explore the medieval castle, the charming old town, and the local artisan shops.\n\n4. **Nora**: An archaeological site that showcases the ruins of an ancient Phoenician city, Nora is set against a beautiful coastal backdrop. Visitors can explore the remains of Roman baths, temples, and mosaics, as well as enjoy nearby Nora Beach.\n\n5. **San Teodoro**: Known for its vibrant nightlife and beautiful beaches, San Teodoro is a popular destination for both locals and tourists. The beaches here, such as La Cinta, are ideal for sunbathing and water sports.\n\n6. **Olbia**: As a gateway to the Costa Smeralda, Olbia offers a mix of modern amenities and historical sites.
