    Which films are nominated for Best Production Design at the 2025 Academy Awards?
answer: >-
    The nominees for Best Production Design are:\n\nThe Brutalist – Production Designer: Happy Massee\nDune: Part Two – Production Designer: Patrice Vermette\nEmilia Pérez – Production Designer: Emmanuelle Duplay\nThe Substance – Production Designer: Jean-Vincent Puzos\nWicked – Production Designer: David Korins\nHappy Massee's work on The Brutalist has been noted for its meticulous attention to architectural detail, reflecting the film's thematic focus. Patrice Vermette's design for Dune: Part Two continues to build upon the expansive and immersive world introduced in the first installment, capturing the stark beauty of the desert planet Arrakis. Emmanuelle Duplay's vibrant and dynamic sets in Emilia Pérez enhance the film's storytelling, creating an immersive experience for the audience. Jean-Vincent Puzos's collaboration on The Substance contributes to the film's unsettling atmosphere, with design elements that underscore its horror themes. David Korins's production design for Wicked successfully translates the beloved stage musical's fantastical elements to the screen, creating a visually enchanting world. \nOSCARS\n\n\n
