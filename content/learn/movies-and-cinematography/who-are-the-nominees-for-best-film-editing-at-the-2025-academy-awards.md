    Who are the nominees for Best Film Editing at the 2025 Academy Awards?
answer: >-
    The nominees for Best Film Editing are:\n\nAnora – Editor: Sean Baker\nThe Brutalist – Editor: David Jancsó\nConclave – Editor: Nick Emerson\nEmilia Pérez – Editor: Juliette Welfling\nWicked – Editor: Myron Kerstein\nSean Baker's dual role as director and editor for Anora showcases his comprehensive vision, seamlessly blending storytelling and pacing. David Jancsó's editing in The Brutalist has been commended for its rhythmic precision, enhancing the film's complex narrative structure. Nick Emerson's work on Conclave maintains suspense and intrigue, effectively capturing the tension of a papal election. Juliette Welfling's collaboration on Emilia Pérez brings a dynamic and fluid editing style, complementing the film's vibrant storytelling. Myron Kerstein's editing in Wicked successfully translates the musical's energy to the screen, ensuring a cohesive and engaging experience for audiences. \nOSCARS\n\n\n
