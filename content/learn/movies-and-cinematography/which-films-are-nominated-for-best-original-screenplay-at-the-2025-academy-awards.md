    Which films are nominated for Best Original Screenplay at the 2025 Academy Awards?
answer: >-
    The nominees for Best Original Screenplay are:\n\nAnora – Written by Sean Baker\nThe Brutalist – Written by Brady Corbet and Mona Fastvold\nEmilia Pérez – Written by Jacques Audiard and Léa Mysius\nThe Substance – Written by Coralie Fargeat\nWicked – Written by Winnie Holzman and Stephen Schwartz\nSean Baker's Anora offers a poignant narrative that has resonated with audiences and critics alike, earning him a nomination for Best Original Screenplay. Brady Corbet and Mona Fastvold's collaboration on The Brutalist presents a complex and thought-provoking story, contributing to the film's critical acclaim. Jacques Audiard and Léa Mysius's script for Emilia Pérez intricately weaves cultural and personal themes, making it a standout in this category. Coralie Fargeat's The Substance delivers a unique and unsettling narrative, showcasing her distinctive voice in cinema. Winnie Holzman and Stephen Schwartz's adaptation of Wicked brings the beloved musical to the screen with fresh energy, capturing the essence of the original stage production. \nOSCARS\n\n\n
