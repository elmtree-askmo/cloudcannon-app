question: >-
    Are there any sample questions available for the Year 12 Chemistry Examination?
answer: >-
    Yes, there are sample questions available for the Year 12 Chemistry Examination in Fiji. The Ministry of Education has provided resources that include sample exam papers to assist students in their preparation for national exams. These sample papers are aligned with the curriculum and can be accessed online for distribution by teachers to students.\n\nAdditionally, students can also download past examination question papers from the Ministry of Education's website. These past papers serve as valuable resources for revision and practice, allowing students to familiarize themselves with the format and types of questions that may appear in the examination.\n\nFor specific sample questions, students are encouraged to refer to the provided sample papers or past year papers, which typically include a variety of questions covering different chemistry strands such as General Chemistry, Investigating Matter, Chemical Reactions, Materials, and Consumer Chemistry.
