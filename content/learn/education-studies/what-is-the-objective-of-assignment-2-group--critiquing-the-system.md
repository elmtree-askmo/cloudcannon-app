question: >-
    What is the objective of Assignment #2: Group – Critiquing the System?
answer: >-
    The objective of Assignment #2: Group – Critiquing the System is to provide students with the opportunity to collaboratively review and critique project ideas produced by their peers. This assignment aims to enhance students' skills in evaluating project descriptions, fostering a deeper understanding of the analytical frameworks used in critiques. Specifically, students will utilize a critique worksheet to guide their analysis and will be required to write a 2-3 page critique based on their findings. This process not only allows students to validate their own techniques but also encourages constructive feedback among peers, thereby enriching the learning experience. The assignment is designed to be completed in groups, promoting teamwork and collaborative learning.
