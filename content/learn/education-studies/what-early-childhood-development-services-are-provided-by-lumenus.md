question: >-
    What early childhood development services are provided by Lumenus?
answer: >-
    Lumenus provides a range of early childhood development services primarily through its EarlyON Child and Family Centres. These services are designed for children aged 0-6 years and their parents or caregivers. The key offerings include:\n\n1. **Play and Inquiry-Based Learning**: The centres create opportunities for children to engage in play and inquiry-based learning, which is essential for their development.\n\n2. **Promotion of Healthy Development**: The programs focus on fostering social, emotional, and communication skills in young children, which are critical for their overall growth.\n\n3. **Support for Parenting**: Lumenus offers resources and support to enhance parenting capacity, helping caregivers to better understand and meet the developmental needs of their children.\n\n4. **Drop-In and Pre-Registered Programs**: These programs encourage healthy parent-child interactions and provide a structured environment for learning and development.\n\n5. **Qualified Professionals**: The centres have qualified professionals available to answer questions and provide information on various topics, including child development, parenting, nutrition, and the importance of play.\n\n6. **Community Connections**: Lumenus facilitates connections to other early years resources in the community, ensuring families have access to a broad range of support services.\n\n7. **Celebration of Diversity**: The programs also emphasize the importance of celebrating diversity within the community, promoting an inclusive environment for all families.\n\nThese services aim to support the healthy development of children and empower families in their parenting journey.
