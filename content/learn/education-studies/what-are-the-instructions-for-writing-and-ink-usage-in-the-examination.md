    What are the instructions for writing and ink usage in the examination?
answer: >-
    For the Fiji Year 8 Examination, the instructions regarding writing and ink usage are as follows:\n\n1. Students must write their Index Number in the space provided in the Answer Booklet.\n2. All answers should be written in the Answer Booklet using either blue or black ink. It is important to note that red ink should not be used.\n\nThese instructions are consistent across various subjects in the Year 8 examinations, ensuring clarity and uniformity in the examination process.
