question: >-
    Are there any accommodations for students with special needs during the exam?
answer: >-
    Yes, there are accommodations for students with special needs during the Fiji Year 8 Basic Science Examination. According to the "Policy on External Examinations and Assessment 2017" issued by the Fiji Ministry of Education, students with special needs are entitled to specific examination provisions. These provisions are designed to ensure that all students, regardless of their disabilities, have equitable access to the examination process.\n\nThe details of these accommodations may include extended time, the provision of additional resources, or adjustments to the examination environment to support the needs of these students. However, the specific types of accommodations available can vary based on the individual needs of the students and the nature of their disabilities.\n\nFor more detailed information on the specific accommodations provided, it would be advisable to refer to the official guidelines or contact the Ministry of Education directly.
