question: >-
    What strategic interventions has the Anusandhan National Research Foundation undertaken?
answer: >-
    The Anusandhan National Research Foundation (ANRF), established by the ANRF Act of 2023, has undertaken several strategic interventions aimed at enhancing India's research and innovation landscape. The Governing Board of ANRF, chaired by the Prime Minister, has identified key focus areas for these interventions, which include:\n\n1. **Enhancing Global Positioning**: ANRF aims to improve India's standing in critical sectors on a global scale, ensuring that the country is competitive in various fields of research and development.\n\n2. **Aligning R&D with National Priorities**: The foundation seeks to ensure that research and development efforts are closely aligned with the national priorities, thereby addressing the most pressing challenges faced by the country.\n\n3. **Promoting Inclusive Growth**: ANRF is committed to fostering an environment that supports inclusive growth, ensuring that advancements in research benefit all segments of society.\n\n4. **Capacity Building**: The foundation focuses on building capacity within academic and research institutions to enhance their ability to conduct high-quality research.\n\n5. **Driving Scientific Advances**: ANRF is dedicated to promoting scientific advancements across various disciplines, including natural sciences, engineering, technology, health, and agriculture.\n\nIn addition to these strategic interventions, ANRF has launched its first two initiatives: the **Prime Minister Early Career Research Grant (PMECRG)**, which invites early career researchers to contribute to transformative research, and the **Mission for Advancement in High-Impact Areas - Electric Vehicle (MAHA-EV) Mission**, aimed at promoting research in the electric vehicle sector.\n\nThese initiatives reflect ANRF's commitment to bridging the gap between academic research and industrial application, thereby fostering a culture of innovation and research excellence throughout India.
