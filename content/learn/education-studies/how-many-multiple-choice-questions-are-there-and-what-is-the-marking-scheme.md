    How many multiple choice questions are there, and what is the marking scheme?

    There are a total of **10 multiple choice questions** in the quiz, and each question is worth **1 mark**, resulting in a total of **10 marks** for the multiple choice section. The quiz format also includes definitions and short essay questions, contributing to a total of **50 marks** for the entire assessment. \n\nThe marking scheme for the quiz is as follows:\n- **Multiple Choice Questions**: 10 questions, 1 mark each (total 10 marks).\n- **Definitions Section**: 6 terms provided, students choose 4 to define, each worth 5 marks (total 20 marks).\n- **Short Essay Questions**: 2 questions provided, students choose 1 to answer, worth 20 marks.\n\nThis structure allows for a comprehensive assessment of students' knowledge and understanding of the course content.
