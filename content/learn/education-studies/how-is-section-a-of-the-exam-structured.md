question: >-
    How is Section A of the exam structured?
answer: >-
    Section A of the Fiji Year 8 Basic Science Examination is structured to include 20 multiple-choice questions, which collectively are worth a total of 40 marks. Students are required to circle the letter corresponding to the best choice for each question in the provided Answer Booklet. This section is designed to assess students' understanding of basic scientific principles and concepts covered in the curriculum. \n\nThe exam allows for a total time of 1Â½ hours, with an additional 10 minutes allocated for reading the paper, ensuring that students have adequate time to review the questions before beginning their answers. All answers must be written in blue or black ink, and the use of red ink is prohibited. \n\nThis structure emphasizes the importance of multiple-choice questions in evaluating students' knowledge and comprehension in a standardized format.
