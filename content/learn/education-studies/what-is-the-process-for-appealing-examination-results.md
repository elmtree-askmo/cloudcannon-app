question: >-
    What is the process for appealing examination results?
answer: >-
    The process for appealing examination results for the Fiji Year 12 Certificate Examination (FY12CE) involves students who are dissatisfied with their marks having the opportunity to request a recount or remark of their examination papers. This process is available for 30 days following the release of the provisional results. The fee for this service is set at $5.\n\nTo initiate an appeal, students typically need to follow these steps:\n\n1. **Access Results**: Students can access their provisional results online through the official examination results website using their unique examination identification number and personal credentials.\n\n2. **Request for Recount/Remark**: If a student wishes to appeal their results, they must formally request a recount or remark within the 30-day provisional period. This request usually involves filling out a specific form provided by the Ministry of Education.\n\n3. **Payment**: A fee of $5 must be paid to process the recount or remark request.\n\n4. **Await Outcome**: After submitting the request and payment, students will need to wait for the Ministry of Education to process their appeal and communicate the outcome.\n\nIt is important for students to act promptly within the 30-day window to ensure their appeal is considered.
