question: >-
    What types of tutoring services are available for science and math students?
answer: >-
    For science and math students, there are several tutoring services available that can provide academic support. Here are the key options:\n\n### 1. **In-Person Tutoring Services**\n- **Locations**: Tutoring services are available at two main locations:\n  - **Greenblatt Library**: Open from 8 AM to 5 PM, with tutoring hours from 10 AM to 7 PM.\n  - **Somerville Campus**: Open from 8 AM to 6 PM, with tutoring starting at 9 AM.\n- **Weekend Support**: New online tutoring services are available on Saturdays and Sundays, primarily at the Greenblatt Library.\n\n### 2. **Types of Tutoring Offered**\n- **One-on-One Sessions**: Students can receive personalized help tailored to their specific needs in subjects like math and science.\n- **Group Sessions**: Collaborative learning opportunities where students can work together under the guidance of a tutor.\n- **Drop-In Hours**: Some tutoring centers may offer drop-in hours for students to get immediate assistance with homework and exam preparation.\n\n### 3. **Online Tutoring Services**\n- **Accessibility**: Online tutoring services are available, allowing students to connect with tutors remotely. This flexibility is particularly beneficial for those who may not be able to attend in-person sessions.\n- **Platforms**: Students can access these services through designated online platforms provided by their academic support services.\n\n### 4. **Additional Resources**\n- **Bartleby**: An online homework help service that covers a wide range of subjects, including math and science. It offers a virtual writing center and one-on-one tutoring through Bartleby Tutor.\n- **AskRose Homework Help**: A free tutoring service operated by the Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, available for students in grades 6-12, providing assistance with math and science homework via phone, email, or live chat.\n\n### 5. **Peer Tutoring and Workshops**\n- **Peer Tutoring**: Students can benefit from peer tutoring programs that provide support from fellow students who have excelled in their courses.\n- **Success Workshops**: Workshops focusing on study skills, time management, and note-taking can also enhance students' academic performance.\n\nThese tutoring services are designed to help students improve their understanding of course material, enhance their study skills, and ultimately achieve academic success in science and math subjects.
