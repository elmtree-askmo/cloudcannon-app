question: >-
    What roles are available for teachers, counselors, and social workers in the education sector at the Impact Fair?
answer: >-
    At the Impact Fair, there are several roles available for individuals interested in the education sector, specifically for teachers, counselors, and social workers. The fair is designed to connect job seekers with organizations that focus on making a positive difference in society. Here are the key roles highlighted:\n\n1. **Teachers**: Opportunities for both full-time positions and internships are available for those looking to enter the teaching profession. This includes various educational settings, from elementary to secondary education.\n\n2. **Counselors**: Positions for school counselors are also featured, where individuals can support students' academic, social, and emotional development. This role is crucial in helping students navigate their educational paths and personal challenges.\n\n3. **Social Workers**: There are roles for social workers who can provide support and resources to students and families, addressing issues that may affect a student's ability to succeed in school.\n\n4. **Instructional Aides**: In addition to the primary roles, there may also be opportunities for instructional aides, who assist teachers in the classroom and support student learning.\n\nThe Impact Fair serves as a platform for networking, learning about job openings, and exploring internship opportunities that can lead to impactful careers in the education sector. It is an excellent opportunity for graduates and those seeking to make a difference in education.
