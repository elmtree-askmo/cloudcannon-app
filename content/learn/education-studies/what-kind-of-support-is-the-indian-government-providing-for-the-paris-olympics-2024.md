question: >-
    What kind of support is the Indian government providing for the Paris Olympics 2024?
answer: >-
    The Indian government is providing extensive support for its athletes in preparation for the Paris Olympics 2024 through various initiatives and funding mechanisms. Here are the key aspects of this support:\n\n1. **Target Olympic Podium Scheme (TOPS)**: The government is committed to ensuring that athletes receive the best possible preparation through the TOPS. This includes engaging world-class coaches and experts to enhance training programs and organizing competitive exposures to improve performance.\n\n2. **Financial Assistance**: The Indian government has allocated significant financial resources, spending over ₹470 crores across 16 sports disciplines. This funding aims to support athletes in their training and preparation for the Olympics. Athletics has received the highest allocation, amounting to ₹96.08 crores.\n\n3. **Large Contingent of Athletes**: India is set to send its largest-ever contingent to the Paris Olympics, consisting of 117 athletes competing in 16 sports disciplines. This diverse team includes both seasoned competitors and first-time Olympic qualifiers, reflecting a broad representation of Indian talent.\n\n4. **Infrastructure and Training Improvements**: The preparations for the Paris Olympics have catalyzed advancements in sports infrastructure and training facilities across India. This transformative phase aims to enhance the overall sports ecosystem in the country.\n\n5. **Support from Organizations**: In addition to government funding, organizations like the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) have also contributed financially, with commitments such as INR 8.5 crores to the Indian Olympic Association.\n\nOverall, the Indian government's comprehensive and targeted approach underscores its commitment to achieving excellence in sports and improving the nation's performance on the global stage at the Paris Olympics 2024.
