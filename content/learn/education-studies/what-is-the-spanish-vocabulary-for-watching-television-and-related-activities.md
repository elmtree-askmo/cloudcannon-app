    What is the Spanish vocabulary for watching television and related activities?
answer: >-
    To discuss watching television and related activities in Spanish, here are some essential vocabulary terms and phrases:\n\n1. **Ver la televisión** - to watch television\n2. **Mirar una serie** - to watch a series\n3. **Ver una película** - to watch a movie\n4. **Hacer zapping** - to channel surf\n5. **Poner la televisión** - to turn on the television\n6. **Apagar la televisión** - to turn off the television\n7. **El control remoto** - the remote control\n8. **La programación** - the programming\n9. **El televisor** - the television set\n10. **La película** - the movie\n11. **El programa** - the program/show\n12. **La serie** - the series\n13. **El documental** - the documentary\n14. **El canal** - the channel\n15. **Binge-watching** - ver maratones (to binge-watch)\n\nThese terms can be useful for engaging in conversations about television, whether you're discussing your favorite shows, planning to watch something, or describing your viewing habits. Additionally, immersing yourself in Spanish-language television can help improve your language skills by exposing you to natural dialogue and vocabulary.
