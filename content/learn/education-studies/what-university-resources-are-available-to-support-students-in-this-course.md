question: >-
    What university resources are available to support students in this course?
answer: >-
    In this course, students have access to a variety of university resources designed to support their academic success, personal growth, and career development. Here are some key resources available:\n\n1. **Academic Success Center**: This center offers free services such as videos, handouts, workshops, and one-on-one support. It also provides Supplemental Instruction (SI) and Peer-Assisted Study Sessions (PASS) for students enrolled in historically challenging courses, ensuring they have the necessary resources to succeed.\n\n2. **Academic Advising**: Students can receive guidance and support from academic advisors who help them navigate their program of study, including choosing or changing majors.\n\n3. **Career Services**: This office assists students with career counseling, job search strategies, and professional development resources, including virtual drop-in appointments and specialized support for students with disabilities.\n\n4. **Student Academic Center (SAC)**: The SAC helps students build college success skills through credit-bearing classes, peer-assisted study sessions, and a video help series. One-on-one support is also available to address specific academic challenges.\n\n5. **Mental Health and Counseling Services**: These services provide support for students' mental well-being, offering counseling and resources to help manage stress and personal issues.\n\n6. **Transformative Learning**: This initiative offers a collection of free tutoring and coaching resources across campus, making it easier for students to find academic support tailored to their needs.\n\n7. **Center for Teaching and Learning**: This center provides academic skills resources, including academic coaching, workshops, and study halls, aimed at empowering students to become effective learners.\n\n8. **Peer Tutoring and Accountability Groups**: Students can find peer tutors for specific courses and participate in accountability groups to enhance productivity and study habits.\n\nThese resources collectively aim to create a supportive environment that fosters academic achievement and personal development, ensuring that students have the tools they need to succeed in their educational journey.
