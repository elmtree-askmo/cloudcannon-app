question: >-
    How can students complete the course evaluations?
answer: >-
    To complete the course evaluations, students should follow these steps:\n\n1. Log into the my.Scranton portal.\n2. On the Home page, locate the display card labeled "Student Course Evaluations."\n3. Click on the Course Evaluation link within that card.\n4. A list of all courses for the current term/semester will appear, along with a status field indicating which evaluations are available for completion.\n\nThis process ensures that students can provide feedback on their courses, which is essential for improving the quality of education and course offerings. Additionally, instructors may encourage students to complete these evaluations by highlighting their importance or offering incentives, such as extra credit, based on the overall response rate.
