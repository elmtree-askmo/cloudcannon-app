question: >-
    Are there specific guidelines for filling out the answer booklet?
answer: >-
    For the Ministry of Education Fiji Year 12 Chemistry Examination 2023, there are specific guidelines for filling out the answer booklet. Here are the key instructions based on the gathered information:\n\n1. **Index Number**: Students must write their Index Number on the top right corner of the answer booklet. This is crucial for identification and grading purposes.\n\n2. **Answering Questions**: All answers should be written in the provided answer booklet. It is important to ensure that responses are clear and legible.\n\n3. **Additional Instructions**: While the gathered information does not specify further details, it is common practice in examinations to use a black pen for writing and a pencil for drawing diagrams. Students should also be aware of any specific instructions provided at the beginning of the examination paper.\n\n4. **Submission**: At the end of the examination, students are required to hand in the answer booklet.\n\nThese guidelines are designed to ensure a smooth examination process and to facilitate the accurate assessment of students' work. It is advisable for students to familiarize themselves with these instructions prior to the examination day.
