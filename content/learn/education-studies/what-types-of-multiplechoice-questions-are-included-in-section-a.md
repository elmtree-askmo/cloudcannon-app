question: >-
    What types of multiple-choice questions are included in Section A?
answer: >-
    In Section A of the Fiji Year 8 Basic Science Examination, there are multiple-choice questions that assess students' understanding of various scientific principles. This section typically includes around twenty multiple-choice questions, which are compulsory and collectively worth 40 marks. Students are required to circle the letter of the best choice in the provided answer booklet.\n\nThe questions in this section cover a range of topics relevant to the Year 8 science curriculum, including but not limited to:\n\n- Basic concepts of matter\n- Principles of energy and forces\n- Earth and space science\n- Living organisms and ecosystems\n- Scientific methods and inquiry\n\nEach question is designed to evaluate the students' knowledge and application of basic scientific concepts, ensuring a comprehensive assessment of their understanding of the subject matter.
