    How do you say 'meditating' in Spanish, and what vocabulary is associated with it?
answer: >-
    In Spanish, the word for 'meditating' is "meditando," which is the gerund form of the verb "meditar." The verb "meditar" translates to "to meditate." \n\nHere are some related vocabulary terms associated with meditation in Spanish:\n\n1. **Meditación** - Meditation\n2. **Reflexionar** - To reflect\n3. **Especular** - To speculate\n4. **Concentración** - Concentration\n5. **Mindfulness** - Atención plena\n6. **Relajación** - Relaxation\n7. **Cuerpo** - Body\n8. **Mente** - Mind\n9. **Energía** - Energy\n10. **Espíritu** - Spirit\n\nThese terms can be useful when discussing meditation practices or incorporating meditation into daily activities.
