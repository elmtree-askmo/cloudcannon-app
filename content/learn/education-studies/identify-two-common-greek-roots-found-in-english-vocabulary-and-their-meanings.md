question: >-
    Identify two common Greek roots found in English vocabulary and their meanings.
answer: >-
    Two common Greek roots found in English vocabulary are:\n\n1. **Bios** (βίος) - This root means "life." It is found in words such as:\n   - **Biology**: The study of life and living organisms.\n   - **Biography**: A written account of someone's life.\n\n2. **Logia** (λογία) - This root means "study" or "discourse." It appears in words like:\n   - **Psychology**: The study of the mind and behavior.\n   - **Theology**: The study of the nature of the divine and religious belief.\n\nThese roots illustrate the significant influence of Greek on the English language, particularly in scientific and academic terminology. Understanding these roots can enhance vocabulary and comprehension of related English words.
