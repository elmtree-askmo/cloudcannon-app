    Were there any partnerships or collaborations involved in organizing the lecture?
answer: >-
    Yes, there were several partnerships and collaborations involved in organizing the lecture. Notably, the lecture was co-hosted by CLN and Hahow for Business, focusing on creating a diverse, equitable, and inclusive (DEI) organizational environment. This collaboration aimed to help businesses understand the importance of cultivating foreign language talents with international perspectives and enhancing cross-cultural understanding and communication skills, which in turn promotes teamwork and boosts corporate competitiveness.\n\nAdditionally, the lecture involved partnerships with various organizations, such as the YS Youth Career Development Center, which provided career support and resources. The collaboration encouraged instructors to develop customized courses and lectures based on their expertise and passion, ensuring the quality and effectiveness of the programs offered.\n\nThese partnerships not only facilitated the sharing of knowledge and resources but also aimed to address educational inequalities and improve overall learning experiences for participants.
