question: >-
    What role does Digital Promise play in promoting AI literacy and digital equity?
answer: >-
    Digital Promise plays a significant role in promoting AI literacy and digital equity through its comprehensive frameworks and initiatives aimed at enhancing educational practices and ensuring equitable access to technology. \n\n1. **AI Literacy Framework**: Digital Promise has developed an AI Literacy Framework that emphasizes the importance of understanding, evaluating, and using AI tools in educational settings. This framework is designed to help learners, educators, and community members engage with AI responsibly and effectively. It encourages educational leaders to integrate AI literacy practices into PK-12 education, ensuring that all stakeholders are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate the complexities of AI technologies. The framework focuses on three Modes of Engagement with AI: Understand, Evaluate, and Use, centering human judgment and justice in the process.\n\n2. **Digital Equity Framework**: In addition to AI literacy, Digital Promise has established a Digital Equity Framework that provides guidelines and policy recommendations to bridge digital divides in K-12 and higher education systems. This framework aims to ensure that all learners have the opportunity to fully participate in the digital world, addressing issues such as access to technology and digital skills. By promoting digital equity, Digital Promise seeks to create an inclusive educational environment where every student can thrive, regardless of their background or resources.\n\n3. **Community Engagement and Ethical AI**: Digital Promise emphasizes the importance of co-designing AI tools and programs with input from educators, students, and community members. This collaborative approach ensures that AI resources are relevant, ethical, and equitable. The organization also advocates for responsible use policies and the development of an AI Bill of Rights for educators, which aims to protect the rights of individuals in the context of AI use in education.\n\n4. **Professional Development and Resources**: Digital Promise provides ongoing professional development and resources for educators to effectively integrate AI tools into their teaching practices. This support is crucial for ensuring that teachers are prepared to leverage AI technologies in ways that enhance learning outcomes and promote equity.\n\nIn summary, Digital Promise is at the forefront of promoting AI literacy and digital equity by providing frameworks, resources, and support that empower educators and learners to engage with AI in meaningful and responsible ways. Their initiatives aim to create a future where technology enhances educational experiences for all students, fostering an inclusive and equitable learning environment.
