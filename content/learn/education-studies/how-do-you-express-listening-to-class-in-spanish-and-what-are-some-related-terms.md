    How do you express 'listening to class' in Spanish, and what are some related terms?
answer: >-
    To express "listening to class" in Spanish, you can say "escuchar en clase." This phrase directly translates to the act of listening during a class session. \n\nHere are some related terms that may be useful in the context of academic vocabulary and daily activities:\n\n1. **Escuchar** - To listen\n2. **Prestar atención** - To pay attention (e.g., "Preste atención al profesor" means "Pay attention to the teacher.")\n3. **Asistir a clase** - To attend class\n4. **Tomar apuntes** - To take notes\n5. **Participar** - To participate\n6. **Estudiar** - To study\n7. **Revisar** - To review\n\nThese terms are commonly used in academic settings and can help enhance your vocabulary related to university life and everyday communication skills in Spanish.
