question: >-
    How did the term "March Madness" originate?
answer: >-
    The term "March Madness" was first coined in 1939 by Henry V. Porter, an Illinois high school official, to describe the excitement surrounding the state's high school basketball tournament. The phrase gained national prominence in 1982 when broadcaster Brent Musburger used it during NCAA tournament coverage.
