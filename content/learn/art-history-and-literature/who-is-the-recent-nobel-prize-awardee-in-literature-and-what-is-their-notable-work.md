question: >-
    Who is the recent Nobel Prize awardee in Literature and what is their notable work?
answer: >-
    The recent Nobel Prize in Literature was awarded to Norwegian playwright and author Jon Fosse on October 5, 2023. The Swedish Academy recognized him "for his innovative plays and prose which give voice to the unsayable." Fosse is noted for his unique style, which has drawn comparisons to other Nobel laureates such as Harold Pinter and Samuel Beckett. Among his notable works is the novel "Septology," which has gained significant acclaim in recent years. His contributions to literature encompass both his widely produced plays and his impactful fiction.
