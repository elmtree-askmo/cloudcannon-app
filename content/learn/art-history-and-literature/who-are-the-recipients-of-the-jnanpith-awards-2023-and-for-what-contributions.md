question: >-
    Who are the recipients of the Jnanpith Awards 2023 and for what contributions?
answer: >-
    The recipients of the Jnanpith Awards 2023 are Jagadguru Swami Rambhadracharya and Gulzar. This prestigious award, which is the highest literary honor in India, was jointly awarded to these two eminent figures for their outstanding contributions to literature.\n\n1. **Jagadguru Swami Rambhadracharya**: He is recognized for his significant contributions to Sanskrit literature. As a scholar and educator, his work has played a vital role in promoting and preserving the Sanskrit language and its rich literary heritage.\n\n2. **Gulzar**: A celebrated Urdu poet, lyricist, and filmmaker, Gulzar is known for his profound impact on Indian literature and cinema. His poetry often reflects deep emotional and social themes, making him a prominent figure in contemporary Urdu literature.\n\nThe Jnanpith Award is presented annually by the Bharatiya Jnanpith and has been awarded since 1961, honoring authors for their exceptional contributions across various Indian languages. The 2023 award marks a significant recognition of excellence in both Sanskrit and Urdu literature.
