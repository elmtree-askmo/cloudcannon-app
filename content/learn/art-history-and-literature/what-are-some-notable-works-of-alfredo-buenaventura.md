question: >-
    What are some notable works of Alfredo Buenaventura?
answer: >-
    Alfredo Santos Buenaventura (1929-), a prominent Filipino composer, conductor, and educator, is known for his significant contributions to contemporary music in the Philippines. He was born in Santa Maria, Bulacan, and studied music at the University of Santo Tomas, Centro Escolar University, and the Gregorian Institute.\n\nBuenaventura's notable works include a variety of compositions that reflect his incorporation of folk and tribal songs, as well as indigenous instruments. Some of his most famous pieces are:\n\n1. **Maria Makiling** - A composition that draws inspiration from Filipino folklore.\n2. **Pasko ng Barangay** - A festive piece celebrating the Christmas spirit in local communities.\n3. **Ang Ating Watawat** - A patriotic song that emphasizes national pride.\n4. **Diego Silang** - An opera that tells the story of the Filipino revolutionary leader.\n\nIn addition to these, Buenaventura composed over 50 religious songs and hymns, showcasing his versatility and dedication to various musical forms. His work has earned him numerous awards, solidifying his legacy in the Philippine music scene. He also served as the dean of the Centro Escolar University Conservatory of Music and was the organist at the Manila Cathedral, further contributing to the musical education and cultural landscape of the Philippines.
