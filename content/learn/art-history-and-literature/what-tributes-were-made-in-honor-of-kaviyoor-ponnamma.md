question: >-
    What tributes were made in honor of Kaviyoor Ponnamma?
answer: >-
    Kaviyoor Ponnamma, a beloved veteran actor in the Malayalam film industry, passed away at the age of 80, leading to an outpouring of tributes from her colleagues and fans. Known for her iconic roles as a mother in over 700 films, Ponnamma's contributions to cinema were deeply cherished.\n\nProminent figures in the industry, including renowned actors Mohanlal and Mammootty, expressed their sorrow and paid their respects. Mohanlal, who portrayed her son in several films, shared heartfelt sentiments, acknowledging the love and warmth she brought to his characters. Other notable personalities such as Siddique, Kunchacko Boban, Manoj K. Jayan, and directors like Renji Panicker and B. Unnikrishnan also attended the memorial services, highlighting her significant impact on Malayalam cinema.\n\nHer funeral was held with full state honors, attended by thousands, including VIPs, reflecting her status and the affection held for her by the public and the film fraternity. The ceremony took place at her residence in Aluva, where her brother lit the pyre, marking a poignant farewell to a cherished figure in the industry. The collective mourning and tributes underscore the legacy she leaves behind as a symbol of maternal roles in Malayalam films.
