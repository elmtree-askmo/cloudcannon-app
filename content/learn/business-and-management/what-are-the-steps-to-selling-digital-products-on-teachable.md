question: >-
    What are the steps to selling digital products on Teachable?
answer: >-
    To sell digital products on Teachable, you can follow these steps:\n\n1. **Create Your Teachable Account**: Sign up for a Teachable account if you haven't already. Choose a plan that suits your needs, as Teachable offers various pricing options.\n\n2. **Set Up Your School**: Once your account is created, set up your school. This includes customizing your school’s branding, such as the logo, colors, and layout.\n\n3. **Create Your Digital Product**: \n   - Navigate to the "Products" section in your Teachable admin dashboard.\n   - Click on "New Product" and select the type of digital product you want to create (e.g., online course, coaching service, or digital download).\n   - Fill in the necessary details, including the title, description, and any other relevant information.\n\n4. **Design Your Product**: \n   - Upload any necessary content, such as videos, PDFs, or other digital files.\n   - Create product graphics to make your listing visually appealing.\n\n5. **Set Pricing**: Determine how much you want to charge for your digital product. Teachable allows you to set one-time payments, subscriptions, or payment plans.\n\n6. **Customize Checkout Options**: If you want to customize how customers purchase your product, you can integrate your Teachable product's checkout URL into an external sales page.\n\n7. **Launch Your Product**: Once everything is set up, publish your product. Make sure to test the purchasing process to ensure everything works smoothly.\n\n8. **Market Your Product**: Use various marketing strategies to promote your digital product. This can include content marketing, social media promotion, email marketing, and leveraging SEO to attract potential customers.\n\n9. **Monitor Performance**: After launching, use Teachable’s analytics tools to track sales, student engagement, and other metrics to understand how your product is performing.\n\n10. **Iterate and Improve**: Based on feedback and performance data, make necessary adjustments to your product or marketing strategies to enhance sales and customer satisfaction.\n\nBy following these steps, you can effectively create and sell digital products on Teachable, leveraging its powerful features to reach a global audience.
