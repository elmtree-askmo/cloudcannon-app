question: >-
    What exhibits and sample documents are provided with the agreement?
answer: >-
    The exhibits and sample documents typically provided with a Managed Service Provider (MSP) agreement can vary depending on the specific program and the parties involved. Based on the gathered information, here are some common exhibits and sample documents that may be included:\n\n1. **Exhibit A: Description of Deliverables** - This exhibit outlines the anticipated staff augmentation (hourly-based) process and the Statement of Work (SOW) for deliverable-based processes.\n\n2. **Exhibit B: Payment Provisions** - This section details the payment terms and conditions associated with the services provided.\n\n3. **Exhibit C: General Provisions** - This exhibit includes the general terms and conditions that govern the agreement.\n\n4. **Exhibit D: Defined Terms** - This section provides definitions for key terms used throughout the agreement to ensure clarity and mutual understanding.\n\n5. **Exhibit E: Participating Addendum** - This document may include additional terms or modifications specific to the participating entities involved in the agreement.\n\n6. **Exhibit F: Approved Subcontractors** - This exhibit lists any subcontractors that have been approved to perform services under the agreement.\n\n7. **Template Request for Offer** - A sample document that outlines how to request offers for services from the MSP.\n\nThese exhibits serve to clarify the obligations, expectations, and legal framework of the agreement, ensuring that both parties have a clear understanding of their roles and responsibilities.
