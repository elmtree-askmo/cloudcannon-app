question: >-
    What initiatives has the Rajasthan government taken to attract investment?
answer: >-
    The Rajasthan government has implemented several initiatives to attract investment in the state, focusing on various sectors and policies. Here are some key initiatives:\n\n1. **Renewable Energy Policy 2023**: This groundbreaking policy aims to attract an estimated investment of ₹4 lakh crore in the state, targeting a total of 90 GW of renewable power projects by 2029-30. The breakdown includes 65 GW from solar, 15 GW from wind and hybrid sources, and 10 GW from hydro and battery energy storage systems (BESS).\n\n2. **Investment Promotion Schemes**: The Rajasthan Investment Promotion Scheme, 2024, along with the Rajasthan Biomass and Waste to Energy Policy, 2023, and the Rajasthan Ethanol Production Promotion Policy, 2021, are designed to create a conducive environment for various industries.\n\n3. **Support for Startups**: The government has allocated over ₹1,100 crore for startups and technological innovation, proposing a ₹100 crore fund-of-funds for equity funding. Additionally, a ₹25 crore corpus fund under the i-Start Fund will allow startups to receive work orders directly from government departments.\n\n4. **Tourism Initiatives**: The tourism sector, which generated USD 28 billion in foreign exchange earnings in FY 2023, is being further boosted by initiatives like the 'Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual Augmentation Drive' (PRASHAD) scheme.\n\n5. **Rising Rajasthan Global Investment Summit**: Scheduled for December 9-11, 2024, this summit aims to showcase investment opportunities in the state, with participation from 26 countries. The event will include strategic sector sessions and one-on-one business meetings to facilitate investment discussions.\n\n6. **New Mineral Policy**: The Rajasthan government is set to unveil a new mineral policy aimed at creating a favorable investment climate to attract significant domestic and foreign investments in the mining sector.\n\n7. **International Outreach**: The state has actively engaged in international investor meets, including a recent delegation to Dubai, to attract foreign investment, particularly from Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries.\n\nThese initiatives reflect Rajasthan's commitment to fostering a business-friendly environment and enhancing its attractiveness as an investment destination across various sectors.
