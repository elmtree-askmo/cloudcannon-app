question: >-
    What were the main MoUs signed during Rajasthan's Investment Summit?
answer: >-
    During the "Rising Rajasthan" Global Investment Summit, which is set to take place from December 9-11, 2023, a significant number of Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) were signed, amounting to a total value of ₹12.5 lakh crore (approximately $150 billion). These agreements were primarily secured during roadshows held in Delhi and Mumbai, with the aim of attracting global investments to the state of Rajasthan.\n\nKey highlights of the MoUs include:\n\n1. **Investment Focus**: The signed MoUs propose projects across various segments, particularly in the renewable energy sector. This includes investments in solar, wind, green hydrogen, hybrid systems, pump storage, battery storage, and green ammonia projects.\n\n2. **Major Contributions**: Notably, Tata Power has committed to an investment of ₹1.2 lakh crore over a ten-year period, which stands out for its size among the agreements.\n\n3. **Job Creation**: The investments are expected to generate approximately 678,000 jobs, primarily in sectors such as renewable energy, education, and cement.\n\n4. **Economic Goals**: The summit aims to boost Rajasthan's economy to reach a target of USD 350 billion within five years.\n\nOverall, the "Rising Rajasthan" summit represents a significant effort by the state government to enhance economic growth and attract substantial foreign and domestic investments across various sectors.
