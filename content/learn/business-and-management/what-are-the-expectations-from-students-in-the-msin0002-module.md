question: >-
    What are the expectations from students in the MSIN0002 module?
answer: >-
    The expectations from students in the MSIN0002 module, titled "Foundations of Management," typically include the following:\n\n1. **Engagement with Course Material**: Students are expected to actively engage with the foundational concepts of management, including historical context, social and economic forces, and the definition and role of management.\n\n2. **Participation in Learning Activities**: Active participation in seminars, case study analyses, and discussions is crucial. This includes contributing to group discussions and collaborating with peers on case studies, such as the leadership case study on PepsiCo.\n\n3. **Development of Strategic Thinking Skills**: Students are encouraged to develop strategic thinking skills, which are essential for understanding and formulating effective management strategies.\n\n4. **Completion of Assignments and Assessments**: Students are expected to complete various assignments, including individual papers and case study analyses, which may involve critical evaluation of real-world business scenarios.\n\n5. **Utilization of Resources**: Students should make use of available resources, such as the Revision App and other learning materials provided for the module, to enhance their understanding and performance.\n\n6. **Attendance and Participation**: Regular attendance in lectures and seminars is often required, as it contributes to a comprehensive understanding of the course content.\n\nThese expectations aim to provide students with a solid grounding in management principles, preparing them for further studies and professional roles in the field.
