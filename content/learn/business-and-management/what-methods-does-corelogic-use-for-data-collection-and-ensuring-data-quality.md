question: >-
    What methods does CoreLogic use for data collection and ensuring data quality?
answer: >-
    CoreLogic employs a variety of methods for data collection and ensuring data quality, which are crucial for maintaining the integrity of its property data services. Here are the key methods used by CoreLogic:\n\n1. **Diverse Data Sources**: CoreLogic collects data from a wide range of trusted sources, including public records, property tax records, Multiple Listing Service (MLS) listings, and proprietary data sources. This comprehensive approach allows CoreLogic to maintain a holistic view of the real estate market.\n\n2. **360 Property Data**: CoreLogic utilizes its 360 Property Data platform to power businesses with extensive property data, insights, and technology. This platform accelerates appraiser workflows through automation, simplifying the data collection process.\n\n3. **Data Enrichment**: The company has acquired a diverse array of data assets, which enhances its data offerings. This data enrichment allows CoreLogic to leverage various datasets to improve the quality and depth of the information provided to clients.\n\n4. **Quality Assurance Practices**: CoreLogic implements quality assurance measures that enable users to electronically review and re-inspect claims, check for errors, and verify compliance with best practices. This process helps in identifying recurring issues and improving overall data quality.\n\n5. **Regular Updates**: The frequency at which data sources are updated is critical to data quality. CoreLogic ensures that its data is regularly refreshed to maintain accuracy and reliability.\n\n6. **Data Quality Standards**: CoreLogic emphasizes the importance of data quality, which refers to the accuracy, completeness, reliability, and fitness of data for its intended use. High-quality data is essential for making informed decisions and deriving actionable insights.\n\n7. **Discovery Platform**: CoreLogic's Discovery Platform integrates over 50 years of property and financial data, covering 99.9% of the U.S. housing market. This platform provides access to high-quality, comprehensive property data packaged as data products, facilitating rapid discovery and integration.\n\nBy employing these methods, CoreLogic ensures that its data collection processes are robust and that the data provided to clients is of high quality, thereby supporting effective decision-making in the property ecosystem.
