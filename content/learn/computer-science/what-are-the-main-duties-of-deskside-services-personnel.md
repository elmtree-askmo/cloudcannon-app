question: >-
    What are the main duties of Deskside Services personnel?
answer: >-
    Deskside Services personnel, often referred to as deskside support technicians or analysts, play a crucial role in providing direct technical support to end-users within an organization. Their main duties typically include:\n\n1. **Installation and Configuration**: They are responsible for the installation, configuration, and testing of hardware and software components on desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.\n\n2. **Troubleshooting**: Deskside support personnel analyze and troubleshoot hardware and software problems reported by users. This includes diagnosing issues and providing solutions to ensure minimal disruption to the user's work.\n\n3. **Customer Service**: Good communication and customer service skills are essential, as they interact directly with end-users to understand their technical issues and provide assistance.\n\n4. **Documentation**: They must properly track service requests and document resolutions in the ticketing system to maintain a record of issues and solutions, which can help in identifying recurring problems.\n\n5. **Collaboration**: Deskside support personnel often work closely with other IT department staff and may assist with tasks that fall outside their immediate expertise, escalating issues to higher-level support when necessary.\n\n6. **Remote Support**: Familiarity with remote access tools and techniques is important, as they may need to assist users who are working remotely or in different locations.\n\n7. **Support for Applications**: They may also provide support for various enterprise and departmental applications, ensuring that users can effectively utilize the software necessary for their roles.\n\nOverall, deskside services personnel are vital for maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of IT operations within an organization, ensuring that users have the support they need to perform their tasks effectively.
