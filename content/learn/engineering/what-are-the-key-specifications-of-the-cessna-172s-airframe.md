question: >-
    What are the key specifications of the Cessna 172S airframe?
answer: >-
    The Cessna 172S is a popular single-engine aircraft known for its reliability and performance. Here are the key specifications of the Cessna 172S airframe:\n\n- **Length**: 27 ft 2 in (8.3 m)\n- **Height**: 8 ft 11 in (2.7 m)\n- **Wingspan**: 36 ft 1 in (11.00 m)\n- **Wing Area**: 174.0 square feet\n- **Wheelbase Length**: 65.0 inches\n- **Propeller Ground Clearance**: 11.25 inches\n- **Minimum Turning Radius**: 27 ft 5.5 in\n\nThe aircraft is powered by a Lycoming IO-360-L2A engine, which produces 180 horsepower. It features a McCauley 2-blade metal, fixed-pitch propeller with a diameter of 76 inches. The Cessna 172S has a maximum ramp weight of 2,558 lbs and a gross weight of 2,550 lbs.\n\nThese specifications highlight the Cessna 172S's design as a stable and versatile aircraft, making it a favored choice for flight training and general aviation.
