question: >-
    What are the future developments or updates expected for Airtable?
answer: >-
    Based on the gathered information, several future developments and updates are expected for Airtable, particularly in 2024. Here are some key highlights:\n\n1. **Airtable AI Enhancements**: Airtable is integrating advanced AI capabilities, including partnerships with IBM watsonx.ai and IBM Granite Models. This will allow users to leverage AI-driven workflows and enhance their productivity across various applications.\n\n2. **New Enterprise Features**: In September 2024, Airtable is set to launch significant features aimed at enterprise users, including the ability to manage millions of records, which is a substantial increase from the previous limit of 50,000 to 100,000 records. This will enable organizations to streamline their processes more effectively.\n\n3. **HyperDB and App Library**: The introduction of HyperDB will allow companies to integrate data from platforms like Snowflake and Databricks, facilitating the creation of bespoke, AI-driven workflows. Additionally, the App Library and App Sandbox features are expected to transform how businesses operate within Airtable.\n\n4. **User Experience Improvements**: Upcoming updates will focus on enhancing the in-base experience, making it easier to navigate and discover automation and interface features. This includes improvements in the user interface to streamline workflows.\n\n5. **DareTable 2024 Conference**: The DareTable 2024 conference, scheduled for October 25-26, 2024, will showcase new features and provide insights into the future direction of Airtable, including discussions on no-code development and innovative tools.\n\nThese developments indicate Airtable's commitment to evolving its platform to meet the needs of both individual users and large organizations, particularly in the realm of AI and data management.
