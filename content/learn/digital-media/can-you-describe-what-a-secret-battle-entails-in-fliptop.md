question: >-
    Can you describe what a 'Secret Battle' entails in FlipTop?
answer: >-
    In FlipTop, a 'Secret Battle' refers to a specific format of rap battle that is typically used during tryouts or special events. Unlike the more common written format, which has become the standard in FlipTop tournaments, secret battles are characterized by their spontaneous nature. In these battles, emcees (rappers) engage in freestyle rap without prior preparation of lyrics, making it a test of their improvisational skills and quick wit.\n\nThe secret battle format is one of several variations within FlipTop, which also includes other styles such as Dos por dos (tag-team battles), Femcee battles (all-female rap battles), and intergender battles. While secret battles were once more prevalent, they have become less common as the written format has gained popularity among participants and audiences alike.\n\nOverall, secret battles highlight the raw talent and linguistic competence of the emcees, showcasing their ability to craft clever insults and engage in witty exchanges on the spot. This format adds an element of surprise and excitement to the FlipTop events, contributing to the dynamic nature of the rap battle scene in the Philippines.
