question: >-
    What are the key events or activities during the Anne Marie D’Amico Day of Kindness and Love?
answer: >-
    The Anne Marie D'Amico Day of Kindness and Love is celebrated annually on April 23, and it serves as a commemoration of Anne Marie D'Amico, a victim of the Toronto Van Attack/Yonge Street Tragedy. This day is marked by various events and activities aimed at promoting kindness and love within the community. \n\nKey events and activities during this day include:\n\n1. **School Engagement**: The Toronto Catholic District School Board (TCDSB) encourages school communities to engage in events, share stories, and participate in activities that honor the spirit of kindness and love. This includes various initiatives led by students and staff.\n\n2. **High Tea with Anne Marie**: An event known as "High Tea with Anne Marie" is organized, which provides an opportunity for community members to gather, reflect, and celebrate Anne Marie's legacy. For instance, in 2023, this event was scheduled for June 4 at The Old Mill, with tickets available until May 23.\n\n3. **Community Involvement**: The day encourages acts of kindness throughout the community, with individuals and groups participating in various charitable activities and initiatives that reflect Anne Marie's positive spirit.\n\n4. **Memorial Award**: The Anne Marie D'Amico Memorial Award is also associated with this day, recognizing individuals who embody the values of kindness and community service. Applications for this award typically close on February 29 of the following year.\n\n5. **Awareness and Fundraising**: The day serves as a platform to raise awareness and funds for the North York Women's Shelter, which supports women and children in need. This aligns with the foundation's mission to honor Anne Marie's memory through humanitarian efforts.\n\nOverall, the Anne Marie D'Amico Day of Kindness and Love is a significant occasion that not only commemorates a tragic event but also promotes a culture of kindness and community support.
