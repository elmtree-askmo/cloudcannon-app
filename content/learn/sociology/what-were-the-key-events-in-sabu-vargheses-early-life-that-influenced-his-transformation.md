question: >-
    What were the key events in Sabu Varghese's early life that influenced his transformation?
answer: >-
    Sabu Varghese, also known as Kunjumon, has undergone a remarkable transformation from being a poacher to a dedicated wildlife conservationist. Key events in his early life that influenced this transformation include:\n\n1. **Involvement in Poaching**: Sabu Varghese was initially part of a poaching gang that operated along the Tamil Nadu-Kerala border. This experience exposed him to the illegal wildlife trade and the detrimental effects it has on the environment.\n\n2. **Switch to Conservation**: At the age of 23, Sabu made a pivotal decision to leave his life of crime behind. This change was marked by his commitment to protecting the forests and wildlife, particularly in the Periyar Tiger Reserve. His dedication and fearlessness in this new role have been significant in his transformation.\n\n3. **Recognition and Awards**: His efforts in conservation have not gone unnoticed. Sabu Varghese has received the prestigious Cricketers for Wildlife Conservation Service Award, which highlights his contributions to forest conservation and eco-tourism. This recognition has further motivated him to continue his work in protecting the environment.\n\n4. **Community Involvement**: Sabu is also a member of the Ex-Vayana Bark Collectors Eco Development Committee (EDC) in Thekkady, which emphasizes his commitment to sustainable practices and community-based conservation efforts.\n\nThese events collectively illustrate Sabu Varghese's journey from a life of poaching to becoming a respected figure in wildlife conservation, showcasing the potential for change and redemption in individuals who have previously engaged in harmful activities.
