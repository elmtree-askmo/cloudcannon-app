question: >-
    Which areas in the United States have the highest concentration of Chinese Americans outside of King County?
answer: >-
    The areas in the United States with the highest concentration of Chinese Americans, outside of King County, include:\n\n1. **California**: This state has the largest Chinese American population, accounting for approximately 40% of the community. Notable areas include:\n   - **San Francisco Bay Area**: Particularly in cities like San Francisco, which has a historic Chinatown.\n   - **Los Angeles County**: The San Gabriel Valley is known for its significant Chinese American population, with cities like Monterey Park having a high percentage of Chinese Americans (43.7%).\n\n2. **New York**: The New York City metropolitan area has the largest Chinese American population outside of Asia, with significant concentrations in:\n   - **Manhattan**: Home to one of the most famous Chinatowns in the U.S.\n   - **Brooklyn and Queens**: Both boroughs have thriving Chinese communities and multiple Chinatowns.\n\n3. **Texas**: Cities like Houston have a growing Chinese American population, contributing to the state's overall demographic.\n\n4. **Hawaii**: This state has the highest concentration of Chinese Americans relative to its total population, with a significant percentage identifying as Asian.\n\n5. **New Jersey**: This state also has a notable Chinese American community, particularly in urban areas.\n\nThese regions reflect historical immigration patterns and the establishment of vibrant Chinese American communities across the United States.
