question: >-
    What are the eligibility criteria for accessing services at Yellow Brick House?
answer: >-
    The eligibility criteria for accessing services at Yellow Brick House primarily focus on women and children who are experiencing gender-based violence or abuse. Here are the key points regarding eligibility:\n\n1. **Target Population**: Women aged 16 years and older, with or without children, who are experiencing abuse from an intimate partner or family member. This includes those who have faced such abuse within the last six months to a year.\n\n2. **Children**: The services also extend to children aged 4 to 15 years old who are affected by domestic violence.\n\n3. **Residency**: Access to services may depend on an assessment of residency and space availability within the shelter.\n\n4. **Intake Assessment**: An intake assessment is required, which can be initiated by calling the crisis line. This assessment helps determine the specific needs and eligibility for the various programs offered.\n\n5. **No Fees**: There are no fees associated with accessing the services provided by Yellow Brick House.\n\nOverall, the organization aims to support individuals who are in crisis due to domestic violence, providing them with emergency shelter, counseling, and various support services to help them rebuild their lives.
