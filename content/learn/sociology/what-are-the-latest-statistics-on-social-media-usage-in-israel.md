question: >-
    What are the latest statistics on social media usage in Israel?
answer: >-
    As of early 2023, social media usage in Israel has shown significant engagement across various platforms. Here are some key statistics:\n\n1. **User Base**: In January 2023, there were approximately 7.06 million active social media users in Israel. This number reflects a slight adjustment from previous years due to revisions in data sources and methodologies.\n\n2. **WhatsApp Dominance**: WhatsApp remains the most popular social media platform in Israel, with over 88% of respondents using it monthly. This highlights its critical role in communication among the Israeli population.\n\n3. **Facebook Usage**: As of December 2023, there were about 6.8 million Facebook users in Israel, which accounted for approximately 72.8% of the total population. The largest user demographic on Facebook is individuals aged 25 to 34.\n\n4. **Instagram Growth**: By January 2023, there were around 4.19 million Instagram users in Israel, reflecting a growth rate of 1.12%. Instagram continues to be a significant platform, especially among younger demographics.\n\n5. **Internet Penetration**: Israel boasts a high internet penetration rate of 90.3%, with 8.22 million internet users reported at the start of 2023.\n\n6. **Demographics**: The social media user demographics indicate that a substantial portion of users are aged 18 and above, with 98% of this age group engaging with social media platforms.\n\n7. **Emerging Platforms**: There is a rising trend in the use of platforms like Telegram and TikTok, particularly among young adults, indicating a shift in social media preferences.\n\nThese statistics illustrate the vibrant landscape of social media in Israel, characterized by high engagement on established platforms like WhatsApp and Facebook, alongside the growing popularity of newer platforms.
