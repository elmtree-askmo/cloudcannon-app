question: >-
    How was gender representation addressed in the 70th National Film Awards?
answer: >-
    The 70th National Film Awards, held on October 8, 2024, addressed gender representation in a significant way. President Droupadi Murmu highlighted the ongoing gender disparity within the film industry, noting that only 15 out of the 85 awardees were women. This statistic underscores the need for increased female representation in Indian cinema. The President's remarks aimed to advocate for greater inclusivity and recognition of women's contributions to the film industry, emphasizing the importance of addressing gender imbalance in such prestigious awards.\n\nThe awards ceremony not only celebrated cinematic excellence but also served as a platform to raise awareness about the challenges women face in the industry, thereby encouraging a dialogue on the necessity for change and improvement in gender representation.
