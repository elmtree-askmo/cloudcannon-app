question: >-
    What is the duration of stay allowed at the Red Door Family Shelter's emergency shelter?
answer: >-
    The duration of stay allowed at the Red Door Family Shelter's emergency shelter varies based on individual circumstances and needs. Some families may stay for just one night, while others can reside at the shelter for as long as six months. This flexibility is designed to accommodate the specific situations of families experiencing homelessness, including those affected by domestic abuse or housing crises. The shelter aims to provide the necessary support to help families regain stability and independence during their stay.
