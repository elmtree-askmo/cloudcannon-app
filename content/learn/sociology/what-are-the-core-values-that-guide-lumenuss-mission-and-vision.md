question: >-
    What are the core values that guide Lumenus's mission and vision?
answer: >-
    Lumenus Community Services is guided by a set of core values that reflect its mission and vision. These values are:\n\n1. **Bold** - Lumenus embraces innovation and courage in its approach to service delivery and community engagement.\n2. **Responsive** - The organization prioritizes being attentive and adaptable to the needs of clients and the community.\n3. **Inclusive** - Lumenus is committed to ensuring that all individuals, regardless of their background, have access to its services and feel welcomed.\n4. **Generous** - The organization fosters a spirit of giving and support, both within its team and towards the communities it serves.\n5. **Humble** - Lumenus recognizes the importance of humility in its interactions, valuing the contributions of clients, families, and partners.\n6. **Trusted** - Building and maintaining trust is fundamental to Lumenus's relationships with clients and the community.\n\nThese values are integral to Lumenus's strategic plan for 2022-2025, which aims to provide excellent care by including clients and families as key partners in the design of services. The organization is focused on creating a seamless, integrated network of mental health, developmental, and community services to reduce systemic barriers and improve access for all individuals and families in Toronto.
