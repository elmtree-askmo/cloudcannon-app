question: >-
    What are the main activities held in the Bambanti Village during the festival?
answer: >-
    The Bambanti Festival, celebrated annually in Isabela Province, Philippines, features a variety of vibrant activities that reflect the agricultural heritage and cultural significance of the region. The main activities held during the festival include:\n\n1. **Bambanti Village**: This agri-ecotourism exhibit showcases intricate giant scarecrows (bambanti) and various agricultural products from different municipalities. The village serves as a central hub for trade and cultural exchange, attracting both locals and visitors.\n\n2. **Cultural Exhibits**: The festival includes cultural exhibits that highlight the talents and traditions of the IsabeleÃ±os, showcasing local crafts, food, and performances.\n\n3. **Music Festivals and Live Performances**: Attendees can enjoy a variety of live performances by local artists, contributing to the festive atmosphere.\n\n4. **Food Festival**: A food festival is held, offering a wide array of local delicacies, snacks, and street food, allowing visitors to experience the culinary heritage of Isabela.\n\n5. **Competitions**: Various competitions take place throughout the week, including street dance competitions that celebrate the festival's theme and the agricultural practices of the region.\n\n6. **Agro-Industrial Trade Fair**: This event features participation from the 34 towns and three cities of Isabela, showcasing agricultural products and innovations.\n\n7. **Grand Street Parade**: A highlight of the festival, the grand street parade features colorful floats and performances, celebrating the community's gratitude for a bountiful harvest.\n\nThese activities not only celebrate the agricultural achievements of Isabela but also foster community involvement and unity among the residents. The festival is a significant event that has gained nationwide recognition, reflecting the rich cultural heritage of the province.
