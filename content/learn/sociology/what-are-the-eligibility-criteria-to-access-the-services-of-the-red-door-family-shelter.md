question: >-
    What are the eligibility criteria to access the services of the Red Door Family Shelter?
answer: >-
    The eligibility criteria to access the services of the Red Door Family Shelter include:\n\n1. **Homeless Families**: The shelter primarily serves families who are experiencing homelessness.\n2. **Immigrant Families**: This includes immigrant families who are awaiting hearing or status.\n3. **Government-Sponsored or Refugee Claimant Families**: Families that are government-sponsored or those who are in the process of claiming refugee status are also eligible.\n4. **Women and Children Fleeing Domestic Abuse**: The shelter provides support specifically for women and their children who are escaping situations of domestic violence.\n\nThese criteria ensure that the shelter can effectively assist those who are most vulnerable and in need of immediate support. If you require further assistance or need to confirm availability, you can contact the Red Door Family Shelter at 416-915-5671 or toll-free at 1-877-338-3398.
