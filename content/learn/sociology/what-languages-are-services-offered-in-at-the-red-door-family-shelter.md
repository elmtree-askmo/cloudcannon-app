    What languages are services offered in at the Red Door Family Shelter?
answer: >-
    The Red Door Family Shelter offers services in several languages, including English, Farsi, Greek, Russian, and Spanish. Additionally, they have interpreters available on call for other languages, ensuring that they can accommodate a diverse range of clients. This multilingual support is crucial for effectively assisting vulnerable populations who may face language barriers.
