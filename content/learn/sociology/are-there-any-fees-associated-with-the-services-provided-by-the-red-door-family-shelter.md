question: >-
    Are there any fees associated with the services provided by the Red Door Family Shelter?
answer: >-
    The services provided by the Red Door Family Shelter do not have any associated fees. This includes emergency shelter, meals, counseling and support, orientation and interpretation, referrals, medical support, and outreach and housing support. However, it is noted that the documents required for accessing these services may vary, and it is advisable to contact the shelter directly for specific information regarding any documentation needed. \n\nFor further assistance or inquiries, individuals can reach out to the Red Door Family Shelter at their contact number: 416-915-5671.
